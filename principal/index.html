The best
gym exercices

Here you will find the best gym exercices for each muscular group, as well as a video tutorial and explanation for each of them. You'll find the repetitions you should do to be optimum and enhance your gains. There will be some alternative exercices as well if it's the case that it is not possible to perform the exercice mentioned. Indeed, the main goal of this website is to give possibilities to gym begginers who don't know about how to start their journey in the gym by giving some useful exercices they can add to their routine
